Services

Career Coaching

Career coaching is the foundation to understanding an individual’s strengths, weaknesses, goals, and commitment to all aspects of his or her career aspirations.

Resume & Cover Letter Writing

A resume and cover letter are essential in highlighting professional accomplishments, experience, and competencies. Crafting a vital resume and effective and concise cover letter will assist in landing the opportunity to interview with a dream company.

Job Search Strategies

In the ever-changing business world, it is imperative to remain current with how to seek jobs and tap into the right networks. The job search process is a full-time job in of itself—we will work together to make this process as current and successful in the smartest way that fits your current commitments.

Acing The Interview

You’ve crafted the perfect resume, cover letter, and landed a coveted interview, now what? Interviewing does not come natural to everyone. Prior preparation and research along with authenticity is fundamental to acing the interview.

Social Media & The Job Search

Social media is a key piece in the job search process. It is also primary in building your online presence and brand. Social media can provide you an opportunity to research companies of interest and learn about the culture and people employed there.

Salary Negotiation

Negotiation is often overlooked and avoided in securing a new position or job due to the fear of the unknown. Negotiation is scary, but it does not have to be. Leaving money on the table or other variables such as paid-time- off or aflexible schedule should not be an option.
